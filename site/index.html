The five rules (they're free — the whole point is following them)

  1. Trade only a confirmed uptrend. Price above the 50- and 200-day averages, averages aligned, higher highs and higher lows. Otherwise: stay out. No exceptions.
  2. Enter without chasing. A pullback that resumes, or a breakout — never a move that's already run far.
  3. Set the stop the moment you enter. Just below the last swing low. Decided before the trade, not during the drawdown.
  4. Size by risk, not by gut. If the stop is hit, lose no more than 1–2% of the account. Ever.
  5. Let winners run; cut the break. Trail the stop up under each higher low. Exit completely when the trend breaks.
The honest part. You can be wrong more than half the time and still come out ahead — because each loss is capped small and winners run. That only works if you follow the rules every time. That's the hard part. That's what Trendkept is for.
