# 05 — Marketing & Customer Acquisition

> The goal: acquire customers **profitably** (file 04 gates) and **legally**
> (file 02 claims rules), then convert them to subscribers and keep them on an
> **owned channel** you can't be banned from.

## 5.1 The golden rule of supplement marketing

> **Every claim is compliant, or it doesn't ship.** Ads, pages, captions,
> influencer scripts, email — all pass the claims checklist (file 02 / file
> 06). The FTC reads ad copy. So do platform reviewers, who ban supplement
> claims aggressively. Compliant copy is also *better* copy: specific, honest,
> trust-building.

## 5.2 Channel strategy — test small, then double down on winners

Don't spread thin. Test 2–3 channels at small budget, kill losers fast, scale
only what clears the LTV:CAC ≥ 3 and payback < 90d gates.

### Owned (highest ROI, can't be deplatformed) — build from day one
- **Email + SMS list.** This is *the asset.* Capture email at every touchpoint.
  Lifecycle flows (welcome, education, refill reminders, win-back) drive
  retention — the master profit variable (file 04). Respect consent/GDPR/CCPA
  and SMS opt-in law (TCPA in the US).

### Earned / organic (slow, compounding, cheap)
- **Content / SEO:** genuinely useful articles around the niche's problem
  (e.g. sleep hygiene), capturing intent search. Compounds for years.
- **Short-form video (TikTok/Reels/Shorts):** founder-led, educational,
  behind-the-scenes (third-party testing, sourcing). Authenticity > polish.
- **Community:** answer questions where your buyer already is.

### Paid (fast, scalable, but must be watched like a trading position)
- **Meta / TikTok ads:** strong for impulse + discovery. Expect strict review of
  supplement claims — keep creative compliant or get rejected/banned.
- **Google Search / Shopping:** captures high-intent buyers ("magnesium
  glycinate subscription").
- **Influencer / affiliate:** powerful and trusted **but you are liable for
  what they say** — give them a compliant script and require the FTC
  **#ad / paid-partnership disclosure**. No script, no post.

## 5.3 The funnel (built to create subscribers, not one-off buyers)

1. **Attract** — organic content + paid ads with a compliant, specific hook.
2. **Capture** — email/SMS via lead magnet (guide, discount) even if they don't
   buy yet.
3. **Convert** — clean product page: third-party tested, CoA published, honest
   structure/function benefit, reviews, clear subscribe-and-save offer.
4. **Onboard** — welcome flow: how to use, what to expect, set expectations
   (reduces early churn and refunds).
5. **Retain** — refill reminders, education, loyalty, easy management.
6. **Win back** — re-engage cancelled/lapsed customers.

> **First purchase is a discounted entry; the profit is in the subscription.**
> Optimise the whole funnel toward subscriber conversion and retention, not
> first-order ROAS alone.

## 5.4 Trust assets (do these — they lift conversion *and* reduce risk)

- **Publish the CoA / third-party test results.** Radical transparency is a
  genuine differentiator in a category full of snake oil.
- **Real reviews** (compliant — no disease claims in testimonials you surface).
- **Clear, honest subscription terms + one-click cancel** (file 02). Trust
  lowers churn and chargebacks.
- **Founder story / face.** People buy ingestibles from brands they trust, not
  faceless stores.

## 5.5 Metrics to watch (your dashboard)

| Metric | Why | Action if bad |
|---|---|---|
| CAC by channel | Profitability | Pause channels above target |
| LTV:CAC | Master health metric | < 3 → fix retention/price before scaling |
| CAC payback days | Cash safety | > 90 → slow down |
| Subscription conversion % | Engine health | Improve offer/onboarding |
| Churn / retention curve | Master profit lever | Improve product + lifecycle msgs |
| Refund + chargeback rate | Risk + processor health | > a few % → investigate product/claims |
| Email/SMS list growth | Owned-asset health | Always be capturing |

> Treat each paid channel like a trading position: defined risk, watch the
> metric, cut it if it stops working. No channel gets unlimited budget on faith.
