FAQ

Is this medicine?

No. Our supplements and vitamins are dietary supplements, and our skincare is cosmetic — neither is medicine. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Speak to your doctor before starting any supplement, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or taking medication.

How does the subscription work?

You receive a fresh supply on your chosen schedule at a discount. You can cancel any time in one click — no phone calls, no hoops.

Where can I see the lab results?

For supplements and vitamins we publish the Certificate of Analysis for each batch. Ask support and we'll point you to your batch's results.

What's your returns policy?

30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
