# Content Strategy

## Workflow
1. Get the niche/audience and business goal (leads, brand awareness, SEO authority).
2. Identify 3-6 pillar topics that map to real buyer questions/search intent.
3. Under each pillar, list 5-10 supporting subtopics (cluster content) that can internally link to the pillar.
4. Suggest a realistic cadence based on the user's stated capacity - don't default to an unsustainable "3x/week" unless they confirm they can support it.
5. Present as a simple table: pillar | subtopics | target keyword | format (blog/video/etc).
