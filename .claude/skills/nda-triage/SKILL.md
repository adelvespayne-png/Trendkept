# Nda Triage

## Workflow
1. Read the NDA and identify: mutual vs one-way, duration/term, definition scope of "confidential information" (is it reasonably bounded, e.g. marked/identified info, or dangerously broad/all info shared), carve-outs (already public, independently developed, required by law), and any non-standard obligations (non-solicit, non-compete sneaked into an NDA).
2. Flag anything outside typical norms (e.g. terms exceeding 3-5 years for most commercial NDAs, missing standard carve-outs, one-sided obligations in a claimed "mutual" NDA).
3. Give a quick verdict: standard / negotiate a few points / concerning, with the specific reasons.
4. Note this is a first-pass triage, not legal advice - recommend real legal review for high-stakes agreements.
