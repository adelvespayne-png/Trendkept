# Mktg Psychology

## Workflow
1. Identify the specific conversion moment (pricing page, cart, email) and current copy.
2. Match relevant principles to that moment - don't just list all of them:
   - Pricing page: anchoring (show highest tier first), decoy pricing
   - Cart: loss aversion, scarcity (only if genuinely true)
   - Onboarding: commitment/consistency (small early asks)
   - Reviews section: social proof (specific, attributed, recent)
3. Rewrite copy applying 2-3 well-chosen principles rather than stacking all of them, which reads as manipulative.
4. Flag ethical lines clearly: fabricated scarcity/urgency or fake social proof are deceptive and shouldn't be produced - apply real triggers to real facts only.
