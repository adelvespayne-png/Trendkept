# Internal Comms

## Workflow
1. Get the project/change, current status, and audience (execs, whole company, specific team).
2. Status reports: lead with overall status (on track/at risk/blocked), then progress since last update, then blockers/risks, then next steps. Keep it scannable - bullets over paragraphs for a busy audience.
3. FAQs: anticipate the real questions people will have (impact to them, timeline, what they need to do), answer directly and plainly, avoid corporate hedging.
4. Match tone to audience - exec updates are terser and metrics-led; team-wide comms need more context and reassurance.
