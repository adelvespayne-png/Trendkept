# Contract Review

## Workflow
1. Read the full contract (upload or pasted text).
2. Go clause by clause and flag: liability/indemnification imbalance, unlimited liability exposure, unusual termination terms (e.g. no exit for convenience), auto-renewal without adequate notice, broad IP assignment, restrictive non-competes/non-solicits, unclear payment terms.
3. For each flag: reference the clause (paraphrased, not verbatim) and explain in plain language what it means and why it matters, plus a concrete alternative phrasing to propose.
4. Summarize overall risk level and the 3-5 highest-priority items to negotiate.
5. Always close with: this is not legal advice, and materially important contracts should get review from a qualified lawyer before signing.
