**Subject:** You're in — here's what The Trend Check is (and isn't)

---

Hi, and thanks for confirming — welcome to The Trend Check.

Quick note on what you just signed up for, so it's exactly what you
expect:

**Every Sunday**, one short email. A written trend-following ruleset runs
over 20 well-known tickers, and I tell you what it says about each —
in an uptrend, nothing confirmed, or trend broken — plus one honest
lesson about risk or discipline. That's it.

**What it isn't:** predictions, hot stock tips, or "buy this now". Nobody
can reliably predict short-term prices, and anyone who says they can is
selling you the feeling. This is a description of what a public ruleset
shows on public data — never advice about your money. What you do with
your own account is always your call.

While you wait for Sunday, three things you might like:

- The free tools — a [position size calculator], [risk-per-trade
  calculator] and a couple more. No sign-up, genuinely useful.
- The whole engine is open source, if you're the tinkering type:
  [github.com/adelvespayne-png/Trendkept]
- You can watch the rules trade a practice account in public — wins,
  losses and boring days, all on the record — from the site.

Reply to this email any time. I read them.
