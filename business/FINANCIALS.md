## Assumptions

- Pricing: £12/mo or £99/yr (40% choose annual) -> blended ARPU £10.50/mo before 4% payment fees.
- Costs: £200/mo fixed + £0.10/subscriber/mo. Owner salary excluded — profit is the owner's pay.
- Business valuation: 3x ARR (small owner-operated SaaS trades at roughly 2-4x).
- Horizon: 60 months. All figures GBP.

## Reading the numbers

The single highest-leverage variable is churn: at 6%/mo the average subscriber stays ~17 months; at 4%/mo, 25 months. Retention work (the daily `manage` habit, the journal, the weekly email) is worth more than any acquisition hack. The conservative scenario never reaches £1M — which is exactly why the plan includes explicit review gates (see PLAN.md §9) instead of blind persistence.
